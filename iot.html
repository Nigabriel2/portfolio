Why this direction

From cabling to circuits

Every hospital and campus network I've worked on is, underneath, a set of devices talking to each other over agreed protocols. Embedded systems and IoT are the same problem at a smaller scale — a microcontroller, a sensor, and a wireless link that has to stay reliable. The transition from "keeping a network up" to "building the devices on it" felt like the natural next step, not a detour.
